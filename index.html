Welcome to Collective Realms Magazine, the world's smallest Fantasy and Science Fiction magazine!

Letter from the Editor

What started with one man creating a blog to feature paladin-related content in 2014, went on to become a thriving internet community known as the "We Are Adventurers Collective". Over the following few years, over a hundred people would go on to become official members of the community, with even more becoming members of "spin-off" blogs.

Now, in 2018, three years later, myself and my co-editor-in-chief are setting upon the enormous task of creating a literary magazine based off of the Collective. We hope that you enjoy what we are creating here at Collective Realms Magazine and look forward to reading the fantastical stories submitted to us.

Thank you for taking part in our adventure.

-Andrew Olvera<
