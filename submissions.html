Collective Realms Magazine is currently open for short story and art submissions.

Fiction Guidelines

Word Limit: 500 - 10,000 words

Genres: Fantasy and science fiction

Rights: Upon acception of your work, Collective Realms Magazine claims Nonexclusive Archival Internet Rights to showcase your work indefinitely (text and audio). We gladly accept work that has been printed elsewhere and do not claim First Rights. By submitting your work to collectiverealms@gmail.com, you are agreeing to these terms. Additionally:

  • The editorial team reserves the right to accept or reject any article submitted for publication, and to edit submitted submissions in any way deemed appropriate and necessary.
  • The editorial team reserves the right to edit articles for length, clarity and conformance with selected style guides.
  • Edited submissions are not routinely returned to authors for their review. However, members of the editorial team, or guest editors, may request additional information or review from authors when they deem necessary.

Story Content:

For now, we are only accepting stories told containing characters contained within the We Are Adventurers' Tumblr community. That being said, no stories shall contain characters that were not created by the author.

We are looking for character-oriented stories. While Collective Realms Magazine does not immediately deny stories with adult themes, works with excessive explicit material will be asked to edit to the satisfaction of our editorial staff.

Fiction Submissions Process

Submit all stories to collectiverealms@gmail.com. You should receive an email from us with 24 hours to confirm your submission. Our general response time is within 7 days.

Be sure to include your name, email address, story title, word count, genre and story. Please submit stories in standard manuscript format and in .RTF, .DOC, or .DOCX format.

Confidentiality

All personal information will never be shared with anyone outside of the editorial staff, except:

  • if compelled by law
  • if threats are made against staff
  • if narrators need assistance with pronunciation (email only)
  • if instructed by the author

Art Guidelines

Collective Realms Magazine features artwork as the cover of the monthly magazine, as well as inside the issue. Our rights are restricted to the cover or within the magazine for that specific issue. All other rights remain with the artist. Artists' bio and links to their gallery will be posted to our website.

Art Submissions Process

Submit all artwork to collectiverealms@gmail.com. You should receive an email from us with 24 hours to confirm your submission. Our general response time is within 7 days.

Be sure to include your name, email address, and piece title.
